NPS 2025 Abstract/Thesis Abstract Submission Guidelines

The template for the abstract and the submission details are given below:

Authors must upload an abstract on the NPS 2025 website in the specified format by 15th February 2025. Use MS Word 2007 or a higher version in ‘.doc’ or ‘.docx’ format. Accepted papers will be presented as poster presentations during NPS 2025.

Ph.D. students who submitted their thesis between 1st January 2024 and 31st December 2024 are eligible for thesis presentation in NPS 2025. Those who are interested should upload the following documents by 15th February 2025:

  1. A five-page summary of the thesis
  2. A certificate from the Ph.D. guide specifying the thesis submission date or award date. (Download sample certificate here)
  3. A list of publications related to the thesis work

Upon acceptance, students must present their thesis work at NPS 2025.

General Guidelines for Submission:

  1. Use the Abstract Submission section in the registration form to submit abstracts.
  2. Accepted file formats: .doc, .docx (Max size: 2 MB for abstracts, 10 MB for thesis abstracts).
  3. File name format: Name_NPS2025
  4. Acceptance/rejection/modification status will be communicated via email.
  5. Resubmission links will be provided if needed.
  6. The best thesis/poster will be awarded.
